Wolfaxen Website — Final PRD (Product Requirements Document)

Version 1.0 — Ready for Build

1. Purpose

Wolfaxen is a consultancy focused on process mapping, optimisation, automation, and AI-powered technical solutions delivered at fair, transparent, non-exploitative pricing.

The website must communicate clarity, capability, and trust — positioned as the practical alternative to overpriced agencies.

2. Target Audience

Small to mid-size businesses

Scaling teams lacking internal technical capacity

Operations, sales, and service teams needing automation

Non-technical founders

Companies considering AI adoption but unsure where to begin

Businesses frustrated with expensive agencies/locked-in platforms

3. Core Value Proposition

“We map your processes, fix inefficiencies, build the automations, integrate the systems, and deliver AI solutions — all for a fair cost.”

5. Pricing Model

Keep it simple, honest, and transparent.

Pricing Structure

Initial process-mapping audit: Fixed price

Project builds: Day rate or per-deliverable

AI agents: One-off build + optional monthly support

Automation builds: Per-flow or per-integration

Web development: Project-based

Support retainers: Low monthly fee (non-exploitative)

Avoid:

Lock-in

Hidden fees

Forced subscriptions

“Enterprise pricing traps”
